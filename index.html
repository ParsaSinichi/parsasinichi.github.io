About Me

I am a graduate student at Ferdowsi University of Mashhad, under the supervision of Dr. Soheila Ashkezari

Also I’m an AI R&D Engineer at C1Tech., where I work on speech and language technologies for Persian voice assistants. I’ve trained a Farsi speech-to-text model that outperforms existing systems like Whisper and helped develop one of the first benchmarks for Farsi STT models. I also fine-tune large language models such as Qwen3, Gemma3 for intent extraction and slot filling, improving performance on low-resource language tasks.

Previously, I was an undergraduate student at Quchan University of Technology, majoring in Computer Science, and a research assistant at the FUM CARE Lab.

My research interests broadly include computer vision, medical image processing, deep learning, and robotics.

During my undergraduate studies, I was fortunate to work with Professor Alireza Akbarzadeh Totonchi, Professor Mohamad-Hoseyn Sigari, and Professor Malihe Javidi.
